The hostels shall function under the overall supervision of administrative control of warden. The policy framework/decisions regarding hostels shall be decided by wardens, wardens and student representatives and mess council. These decisions will be implemented by the wardens the decision of warden in their respective jurisdictions shall be final. Apart from these rules the warden can issues further directives in the interest of smooth functioning of the hostel affairs after due deliberations in the regular meeting of warden

Roles and responsibilities

  • To create an overall disciplined environment in the hostel
  • To provide them infrastructure conducive for their growth
  • To organize various culture and sports events in the hostel to bring out creative abilities of students.
  • To create an environment so that a fleeing of homesickness is not there amongst the students
  • The committee shall create a positive ambience in hostels
  • To provide good healthy and hygienic food
  • Proper maintenance of hostel and mess and to have a check on quality of food
